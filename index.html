about Us

Established in 2014 by Mr Mohammed Bin Quasem, Incredible Fashions Ltd. is an apparel manufacturer dedicated to creating employment opportunities in a safe and healthy working environment. The factory specializes in high- fashion knitted tops and offers a wide range of fabrics. The fabric base includes 100% Cotton, Viscose, Viscose/Lycra, Modal, and Bamboo with weights ranging from 90 to 250 gsm.

Our mission is to create high-quality knitwear products for export to global retailers while creating employment for Bangladeshi workers. We aim to create and develop long- lasting relationships with customers who share our values and philosophy.
